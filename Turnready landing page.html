How It Works

From Checkout to 5 Stars
in One Flow.

1
Guest checks out
A turnover is created for your property. You assign a cleaner with one tap — they're notified instantly.
2
Cleaner accepts & gets to work
They follow the master checklist, document each room with video, and mark inventory levels as they go.
3
You review & approve
See every task, every video, every supply level before you approve. Reject with a reason if anything needs fixing.
4
Payment releases automatically
Approve and Stripe handles the rest. Your cleaner gets paid directly. No manual transfers needed.
5
Guest arrives to a 5-star property
Every turnover documented, every standard met. That's how 5-star reviews happen consistently.
